Tierra Burrito

The best Burritos I've ever tried, highly recomended.

A great place with an even greater service! Very fast and attentive staff and very tasty food. Though you are mostly going to find burritos and simple finger food to share, you can create your own burrito, choosing the ingredients of your liking, making the place suitable for people on a special diet (eg. vegetarians or vegans).

The prices are also quite reasonable, considering the quality and the size of the burritos!
